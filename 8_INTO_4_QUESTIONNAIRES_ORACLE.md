# Dating App Compatibility Questionnaires (4 Consolidated Categories)

**Consolidated from:** 8_QUESTIONNAIRES_ORACLE.md (164 questions → 80 questions)
**Based on:** DEEP_RESEARCH.md (The Science of Romantic Compatibility, Relationship Satisfaction, and Marriage Longevity)
**Completion order:** Values & Lifestyle → Emotional Foundation → Communication & Commitment → Passion & Partnership

---

## Design Principles

These questionnaires follow evidence-based design prioritizing:

1. **Dealbreaker identification** — Values & Lifestyle screens for non-negotiable mismatches early
2. **Emotional foundation** — Attachment & regulation determine stress response patterns
3. **Repair capacity** — Communication patterns predict long-term satisfaction
4. **Relationship glue** — Appreciation, sexual satisfaction, and friendship sustain partnerships
5. **Behavioral items over abstracts** — "When I feel criticized, I tend to..." is more predictive than "I value communication"
6. **Scenario-based** — Real-world situations reveal stress reactions better than ideals

## Response Format Reference

- **single** — multiple choice, choose 1
- **multi** — select all that apply
- **slider** — scale of 1–10 with labeled anchors
- **range** — numeric range
- **likert** — 5-point agreement scale (Strongly Disagree · Disagree · Neutral · Agree · Strongly Agree)

## Matching Weight Reference

- **Critical** — high-weight for matching; major mismatches drive strong penalties
- **Standard** — normal weight; shapes "fit" gradients and conversation prompts

## Questionnaire 1: Values & Lifestyle Compatibility

**Combines:** Values & Life Goals (Q3) + Lifestyle Compatibility & Equity (Q7)

### What this measures

Core values alignment (religion, politics, children, relationship model, location), equity expectations, substance use, and daily lifestyle compatibility. Values show strong assortative mating (politics r = 0.58) and drive "perpetual problems" if mismatched. Inequity breeds resentment, especially during parenthood.

**Importance weight: VERY HIGH for initial compatibility; VERY HIGH for long-term success**
