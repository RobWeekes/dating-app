# Dating App Compatibility Questionnaires

Based on research from "7 Predictors of Long-Term Relationship Success" (Preston Ni, 2013) and peer-reviewed studies on relationship compatibility, these questionnaires are designed to assess partner compatibility across multiple dimensions for both casual/short-term and long-term/marriage relationship goals.

---

## Overview of Compatibility Dimensions

Based on Preston Ni's research and supporting peer-reviewed studies:

1. **Physical Intimacy** — Sexual attraction, physical affection preferences, intimacy frequency expectations
2. **Emotional Intimacy** — Vulnerability, emotional openness, empathy, support, secure attachment
3. **Intellectual Compatibility** — Shared interests, values, intellectual engagement, worldviews
4. **Shared Activities & Lifestyle** — How couples spend time, leisure preferences, lifestyle alignment
5. **Financial Values Alignment** — Money management, spending/saving priorities, financial transparency
6. **Parenting Philosophy Alignment** — Desires for children, parenting approach, family values

## Scoring Guide for Long-Form Questionnaires

### How to Use These Questionnaires:

**For Dating Website Implementation:**

1. **Casual/Short-Term Users:**

   - Recommend SHORT FORM (10 Q) or MEDIUM FORM (25 Q)
   - Captures physical attraction, communication style, and explicit intentions
   - Focuses on compatibility for shorter-term relationships

2. **Long-Term/Marriage Users:**

   - Recommend MEDIUM FORM (35 Q) or LONG FORM (100 Q)
   - Comprehensive assessment of lifetime compatibility
   - Tests shared values, conflict resolution, life goals

3. **Matching Algorithm:**

   - Must/Essential answers indicate non-negotiables
   - Should/Very Important answers indicate strong preference
   - Could/Nice to have indicates flexibility
   - Use weighted scoring: Must = 3 points, Should = 2 points, Could = 1 point

4. **Compatibility Scoring:**
   - Compare responses within each category
   - Highest compatibility: matching answer levels (both Must, both Should, etc.)
   - Moderate compatibility: adjacent answer levels (Must vs. Should)
   - Lower compatibility: distant answer levels (Must vs. Could)

### Research-Based Compatibility Factors:

**For Long-Term Success (per research):**

- Secure attachment style (both partners)
- Shared core values
- Quality communication
- Financial compatibility
- Sexual satisfaction
- Commitment to growth
- Healthy conflict resolution

**Major Risk Factors:**

- Lack of commitment
- Poor communication
- Insecure attachment (especially anxious)
- Financial misalignment
- Unresolved conflict patterns
