# Essential Questionnaire

A comprehensive personality and lifestyle questionnaire that focuses on core personality traits, values, lifestyle preferences, and relationship timeline expectations. Designed to give users and matches a deep understanding of personality compatibility across 9 key dimensions.

---

## Overview

**Type:** Personality & Lifestyle Profile Questionnaire  
**Questions:** 27 total (mix of single-select, multi-select, and slider scales)  
**Estimated Time:** 15-20 minutes  
**Format:** Single-page form with 9 sections

---

## Coverage Dimensions

The Essential Questionnaire assesses compatibility across these 9 dimensions:

1. **Love & Connection** — Love languages and emotional expression preferences
2. **Values & Background** — Political compass and family birth order
3. **Sleep & Personality** — Sleep patterns, personality archetypes, social inclination
4. **Humor & Laughter** — Sense of humor and laugh frequency
5. **Entertainment & Hobbies** — Music, activities, entertainment, and hobby preferences
6. **Lifestyle & Habits** — Socialization frequency, drinking, smoking, drugs, pets
7. **Personality & Communication** — Personality era, conflict style, problem-solving approach
8. **Finances & Family** — Financial habits and family relationships
9. **Relationship Timeline** — Expectations for dating duration and engagement timeline

## Scoring Categories (for Compatibility Algorithm)

Once responses are collected, these dimensions can be scored:

### 1. **Social Compatibility** (Weight: 0.15)
- Source: socialInclination, socializationFrequency, hobbies, entertainment
- Range: 0-100

### 2. **Lifestyle Compatibility** (Weight: 0.12)
- Source: indoorOutdoor, entertainment, outdoorActivities, drinkingHabits, smoking, pets
- Range: 0-100

### 3. **Communication Style** (Weight: 0.10)
- Source: conflictStyle, problemHandling, laughFrequency
- Range: 0-100

### 4. **Personality Match** (Weight: 0.12)
- Source: personalityArchetype, personalityEra, senseOfHumor
- Range: 0-100

### 5. **Values Alignment** (Weight: 0.15)
- Source: politicalCompass, familyRelationship, financialHabits
- Range: 0-100

### 6. **Entertainment/Interest Compatibility** (Weight: 0.12)
- Source: musicalTastes, entertainment, hobbies, outdoorActivities
- Range: 0-100

### 7. **Emotional Compatibility** (Weight: 0.12)
- Source: loveLangs, senseOfHumor, conflictStyle
- Range: 0-100

### 8. **Relationship Readiness** (Weight: 0.12)
- Source: datingDuration, engagementDuration, personalityEra
- Range: 0-100
